PoE module

PoE module يعني نقل data وDC power على نفس Ethernet cable لتغذية devices مثل cameras، access points، readers، intercoms، وIoT sensors.

PoE module delivers Ethernet data and DC power on the same copper cable to devices such as cameras, wireless access points, readers, intercoms, and sensors.

Start Quick Check
VISUALS

Technical Visuals

01

Quick Definition

في PoE يوجد PSE مثل switch أو injector، وPD مثل camera أو AP. التصميم لا يكتفي بعدد ports؛ يجب حساب per-port class، total budget، cable loss، وUPS runtime.

PoE has a PSE, such as a switch or injector, and a PD, such as a camera or access point. Design must check per-port class, total power budget, cable loss, and UPS runtime.

02

Why It Matters

لو power budget غير كاف، الأجهزة قد تعمل أثناء الاختبار ثم تفصل عند تشغيل IR أو heater أو PTZ أو peak load.

If the power budget is weak, devices may pass a basic test but fail when IR, heaters, PTZ motors, or peak loads turn on.

03

Where It Appears

يظهر في network switch schedule، CCTV/AP/reader load list، rack power calculation، UPS sizing، cable schedule، وcommissioning PoE load test.

You meet it in switch schedules, CCTV/AP/reader load lists, rack power calculations, UPS sizing, cable schedules, and PoE load-test reports.

04

Practical Examples

  • Check the device maximum wattage, not only typical consumption.
  • Confirm the PoE standard/class supported by both switch port and powered device.
  • Add total switch power budget and UPS runtime, especially for cameras with IR or heaters.
  • Verify copper cable category, length, bundle heat rise, and patching quality.
DESIGN

Design Considerations

في التصميم، احسب max load لكل device، reserve لكل switch، UPS autonomy، cable length، وspare capacity للتوسعات.

In design, calculate maximum load per device, switch reserve, UPS autonomy, cable length, and spare capacity for expansion.

SITE

Installation Considerations

في الموقع، راجع label لكل port، patching، cable test، distance، وأن الـ device موصل على port يدعم الـ class المطلوب.

On site, check port labels, patching, cable test results, distance, and that the device is on a port supporting the required class.

TEST

Testing And Commissioning

اختبر power draw، link speed، reboot under load، IR/night mode، alarm load، وUPS runtime عند فصل mains.

Test power draw, link speed, reboot under load, IR/night mode, alarm load, and UPS runtime during mains failure.

MISTAKES

Common Mistakes

  • Counting ports but forgetting the shared switch power budget.
  • Using typical wattage instead of maximum wattage.
  • Ignoring distance, patch panels, poor termination, or cable-bundle heat.
TROUBLESHOOT

Troubleshooting Scenario

لو device يعيد تشغيل نفسه، راجع switch budget، negotiated class، cable length، patching، firmware logs، وحالة load مثل IR أو heater.

If a device reboots, check switch budget, negotiated class, cable length, patching, logs, and peak loads such as IR or heaters.
