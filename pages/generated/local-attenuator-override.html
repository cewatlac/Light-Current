Local attenuator override

Local attenuator override جزء من audio path يبدأ من source أو microphone، ثم processing/amplification، ثم speaker zones أو voice alarm outputs.

Local attenuator override belongs to the audio path from source or microphone through processing and amplification to speaker zones or voice-alarm outputs.

Start Quick Check
VISUALS

Technical Visuals

01

Quick Definition

افهمه من خلال signal level، zone، power، impedance/100V line، SPL، intelligibility، وpriority مثل emergency paging.

Understand it through signal level, zone, power, impedance or 100V line, SPL, intelligibility, and priority such as emergency paging.

02

Why It Matters

الصوت قد يعمل لكن يكون غير مفهوم بسبب SPL، reverberation، zoning، أو priority configuration.

Audio may work yet be unusable because of SPL, reverberation, zoning, or priority configuration.

03

Where It Appears

يظهر في speaker layout، amplifier schedule، zone matrix، cable schedule، SPL/STI test، وvoice evacuation cause-and-effect.

You meet it in speaker layouts, amplifier schedules, zone matrices, cable schedules, SPL/STI tests, and voice-evacuation cause-and-effect.

04

Practical Examples

  • Match speaker tap wattage and amplifier capacity with spare headroom.
  • Coordinate zones with building areas and emergency priorities.
  • Check microphone, DSP, amplifier, speaker line, and end-of-line monitoring.
  • During testing, record SPL, intelligibility, zone selection, and priority override.
DESIGN

Design Considerations

في التصميم، حدد zones، SPL target، intelligibility، amplifier loading، cable loss، priority، وbackup power.

In design, define zones, SPL target, intelligibility, amplifier loading, cable loss, priority, and backup power.

SITE

Installation Considerations

في الموقع، راجع speaker tap setting، polarity، line isolation، cable route، amplifier rack، وlabels.

On site, check speaker tap settings, polarity, line isolation, cable route, amplifier rack, and labels.

TEST

Testing And Commissioning

اختبر zone paging، background music، emergency priority، SPL/STI، fault monitoring، وbattery/backup operation.

Test zone paging, background music, emergency priority, SPL/STI, fault monitoring, and backup operation.

MISTAKES

Common Mistakes

  • Sizing amplifiers without speaker tap totals and headroom.
  • Testing sound presence without intelligibility.
  • Ignoring priority and emergency override logic.
TROUBLESHOOT

Troubleshooting Scenario

لو zone صوته ضعيف أو مشوش، افحص tap setting، polarity، amplifier channel، cable loss، DSP gain، وspeaker placement.

If a zone is weak or distorted, check tap setting, polarity, amplifier channel, cable loss, DSP gain, and speaker placement.
